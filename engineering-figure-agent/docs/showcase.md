# Showcase

Three examples that communicate the project promise quickly: concept figures through image mode, exact plots through local rendering, and dense engineering overviews with figure briefs.

## 1. Concept Figure: Cooperative Object Tracking

![Cooperative object tracking](examples/cooperative-object-tracking-2k-1.png)

- Mode: `image`
- Brief: [examples/briefs/cooperative-object-tracking.md](examples/briefs/cooperative-object-tracking.md)
- Prompt: [examples/cooperative-object-tracking-prompt.txt](examples/cooperative-object-tracking-prompt.txt)
- Why it matters: shows how a figure brief can become a clean engineering workflow diagram with readable modules and arrows.

## 2. Exact Plot: Benchmark And Efficiency
