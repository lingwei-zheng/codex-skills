# Geography Domain Glossary

Default domain reference for geography-oriented papers, especially human geography, geographic information science (GIScience), and health geography. Use this glossary when choosing bilingual terminology, search keywords, reviewer personas, and discipline register.

## Core Fields

| English | Simplified Chinese | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Geography | 地理学 | Broad discipline |
| Human Geography | 人文地理学 | Society, space, place, and environment |
| Geographic Information Science (GIScience) | 地理信息科学 | Theory and methods behind GIS, spatial data, and spatial analysis |
| Geographic Information Systems (GIS) | 地理信息系统 | Tools and systems for spatial data capture, analysis, and visualization |
| Health Geography | 健康地理学 | Spatial dimensions of health, disease, care, and wellbeing |
| Medical Geography | 医学地理学 | Often overlaps with health geography; may emphasize disease ecology and medical services |
| Urban Geography | 城市地理学 | Urbanization, urban form, governance, mobility, inequality |
| Population Geography | 人口地理学 | Spatial population distribution, migration, demography |
| Environmental Geography | 环境地理学 | Human-environment interactions and environmental change |

## Key Concepts

| English | Simplified Chinese | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Space | 空间 | Analytical dimension; distinguish from place |
| Place | 地方 | Meaningful and experienced location |
| Scale | 尺度 | Local, regional, national, global; also analytical scale |
| Spatial Inequality | 空间不平等 | Uneven distribution of resources, risks, or outcomes |
| Spatial Accessibility | 空间可达性 | Access to services or opportunities across space |
| Spatial Justice | 空间正义 | Normative framing of uneven spatial arrangements |
| Spatial Autocorrelation | 空间自相关 | Nearby observations are statistically related |
| Spatial Heterogeneity | 空间异质性 | Relationships vary across space |
| Place Effects | 地方效应 | Effects associated with neighborhood/place context |
| Mobility | 流动性 | Movement of people, goods, information, or disease |
| Built Environment | 建成环境 | Human-made physical surroundings |
| Neighborhood Effects | 邻里效应 | Contextual effects of local environments |

## GIScience and Methods

| English | Simplified Chinese | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Spatial Analysis | 空间分析 | General family of analytical methods |
| Geocoding | 地理编码 | Converting addresses or place names into coordinates |
| Remote Sensing | 遥感 | Earth observation from sensors/platforms |
| Network Analysis | 网络分析 | Often used for transport and service accessibility |
| Kernel Density Estimation | 核密度估计 | Common method for spatial concentration |
| Spatial Regression | 空间回归 | Regression models accounting for spatial dependence |
| Geographically Weighted Regression (GWR) | 地理加权回归 | Local regression model for spatial nonstationarity |
| Spatial Interaction Model | 空间相互作用模型 | Models flows between origins and destinations |
| Location-Allocation Model | 选址-分配模型 | Facility siting and service area optimization |
| Modifiable Areal Unit Problem (MAUP) | 可变空间单元问题 | Results vary by aggregation units |

## Writing Register

- Keep specialized method names in English when they are standard in the target literature, adding Simplified Chinese equivalents on first mention when useful.
- Prefer "spatial", "place-based", "scale-sensitive", "contextual", and "geographically explicit" when these describe the actual design.
- Avoid treating geography as only mapping; foreground spatial theory, place, scale, and method when relevant.
